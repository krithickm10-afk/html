Facts About Dogs

Dogs have a sense of time. Its been proven that they know the difference between a hour and five. If conditioned to, they can predict future events, such as regular walk times.

Dogs are incredibly loyal, intelligent, and versatile animals, often called "Man's best friend" for their deep bond with humans, serving as companions, protectors, and helpers in roles from therapy to police work; they provide unconditional love, reduce stress, and encourage activity, making them beloved family members, despite requiring care like walks, proper food, and vet visits.
